What is Diceware?

Diceware is a method for creating passphrases that combines several random words to create a password that is both secure and memorable.

Example: Horse-Battery-Staple-Correct is much easier to remember than Tr0ub4dor&3 while being more secure.

Why is it secure?

  • 5 random words = about 64 bits of entropy
  • Impossible to guess by brute force
  • Resistant to dictionary attacks
