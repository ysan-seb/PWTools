Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PWTools. We take your privacy very seriously. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use our services.

2. Data Collection

All our tools work locally in your browser

Data we DO NOT collect:

  • Generated or tested passwords
  • Diceware passphrases
  • Base64 encoded/decoded content
  • Formatted JSON data
  • Generated QR codes
  • Personal information (weight, height for BMI)
  • Texts analyzed by word counter

All operations are performed client-side, directly in your browser. No data is transmitted to our servers.

3. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies for:

  • Analytics: Google Analytics to understand site usage (anonymized data)
  • Advertising: Google AdSense to display relevant ads

You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to personalize ads based on your visits to this site and other websites. You can disable ad personalization by visiting Google Ads settings .

5. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to analyze our site usage. Collected data is anonymized and includes: visited pages, visit duration, approximate location (country/city), device used. You can disable Google Analytics with the Google Analytics Opt-out extension.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. The only data shared is with Google (Analytics and AdSense) as part of their services.

7. Security

All PWTools pages are served via HTTPS to ensure a secure connection. Since all operations are performed locally, your sensitive data never leaves your device.

8. Your Rights

In accordance with GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access to your data
  • Right of rectification
  • Right to erasure
  • Right to restriction of processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object

To exercise your rights, contact us at: privacy@pwtools.com

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. The last update date is indicated at the top of this page.

10. Contact

For any questions regarding this privacy policy, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@pwtools.com
  • Contact page: contact.html
