Frequently Asked Questions

How to create a strong password?

A strong password must contain at least 12 characters, mix uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. Avoid dictionary words and personal information. Our generator creates random passwords that meet all these criteria.

Are my passwords stored?

No, absolutely not. All passwords are generated locally in your browser. No data is sent to our servers. Your security and privacy are 100% guaranteed.

What password length should I choose?

We recommend a minimum of 16 characters for optimal security. For critical accounts (email, banking), opt for 20 characters or more with all character types enabled.
